Academic Excellence
We offer a comprehensive curriculum that spans the arts, sciences, humanities, and technology. We are committed to providing an education that is both challenging and supportive, helping each student achieve their academic goals.

Extracurricular Activities
Education at Code with Guru goes beyond the classroom. We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, music, drama, and various clubs and organizations. These activities provide students with opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships. We encourage all students to participate and take advantage of the diverse experiences available to them.

Community and Values
At the heart of Code with Guru is our vibrant and inclusive community. We value diversity and strive to create a culture of respect and kindness. Our school community is built on the principles of integrity, compassion, and responsibility. We believe that these values are essential to personal growth and success, both in school and in life.
